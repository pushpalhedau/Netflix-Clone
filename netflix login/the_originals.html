The Originals

DESCRIPTION: The Originals, a spin-off of The Vampire Diaries, centers
on three of the Mikaelson siblings: Klaus (Joseph Morgan), Elijah (Daniel Gillies),
and Rebekah (Claire Holt). The Mikaelson family are also commonly known as
"the Originals" due to the fact that they are the first vampires ever to exist.
Klaus is the son of a witch and a werewolf, and his quest to unleash his hybrid
nature was detailed in The Vampire Diaries. The backdoor pilot, "The Originals",
which aired on April 25, 2013, also revealed that the werewolf Hayley (Phoebe Tonkin),
with whom Klaus had a drunken one-night stand, is pregnant with his child.
